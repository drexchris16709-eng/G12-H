This section is designed to provide students with quick access to important announcements, reminders, attendance records, class officers, learning modules, and other useful resources. This platform allows students to stay updated with the latest classroom activities. Teachers and class officers can post announcements, reminders about assignments, upcoming quizzes, examinations, and school events. By checking this page regularly, every student can remain informed about important deadlines and responsibilities.
