Welcome to Graceful Hopper

Welcome to the official Graceful Hopper class page. This section is designed to provide students with quick access to important announcements, reminders, attendance records, class officers, learning modules, and other useful resources. The goal of this page is to keep every student informed and organized throughout the school year. This platform allows students to stay updated with the latest classroom activities. Teachers and class officers can post announcements, reminders about assignments, upcoming quizzes, examinations, and school events. By checking this page regularly, every student can remain informed about important deadlines and responsibilities. The Attendance section helps students monitor their attendance records and encourages punctuality. The Cleaners section contains the classroom cleaning schedule to ensure that responsibilities are shared equally among classmates. The Officers section introduces the elected class officers and their assigned roles, making it easier for students to know whom to approach whenever assistance is needed. The Modules section serves as a storage area for learning materials, assignments, and educational resources. Students can easily review lessons and download necessary files whenever they need them. This helps make learning more convenient both inside and outside the classroom. Our objective is to create a simple, organized, and user-friendly environment where students can access all important class information in one place. Through teamwork, responsibility, and effective communication, Graceful Hopper aims to build a productive and supportive learning community. Thank you for visiting the Graceful Hopper class page. We hope this platform helps every student become more organized, responsible, and successful throughout the academic year. Continue scrolling to explore more content and features that will be added in future updates.

Rules & Regulations

1. Wear the complete and proper school uniform every day.
Laging magsuot ng kumpleto at tamang school uniform. Ang polo ay dapat naka-tuck in sa lahat ng oras. Ang sinumang hindi susunod ay magbabayad ng ₱15 sa Treasurer.

2. Do not be late, even if you have an excuse letter.
Ugaliing pumasok nang maaga. Ang oras mula 1:00 PM hanggang 1:10 PM ay ituturing na late. Ang mahuhuling late ay maglilinis pagkatapos ng klase kahit hindi siya naka-assign bilang cleaner.

3. Do not throw trash anywhere.
Panatilihing malinis ang silid-aralan. Ang sinumang mahuhuling nagtatapon ng basura kung saan-saan ay siyang magtatapon ng basura pagkatapos ng klase. Kapag hindi ito ginawa, magbabayad siya ng ₱10 sa Treasurer.

4. Always follow the instructions of the class officers.
Igalang at sundin ang mga tagubilin ng mga opisyal ng klase. Ang hindi susunod ay magbabayad ng ₱1 sa Treasurer.

5. Do not bring dangerous items to the classroom.
Bawal magdala ng anumang mapanganib na bagay sa loob ng silid-aralan. Ang mahuhuli ay pagmumultahin ng ₱10 at maaaring i-refer sa Guidance Office.

6. Excuse letters must include a photocopy of a parent's valid ID with signature.
Ang excuse letter ay dapat may kasamang photocopy ng valid ID ng magulang na may pirma bilang patunay ng kanilang pahintulot. Ang hindi makakasunod ay maglilinis pagkatapos ng klase at magbabayad ng ₱10 sa Treasurer.

7. Always follow the instructions of officers and teachers.
Sundin ang lahat ng tagubilin ng mga opisyal at guro. Ang sinumang mahuhuling hindi sumusunod o nagsisinungaling ay magbabayad ng ₱20 sa Treasurer.

8. No vandalism inside the classroom.
Bawal magsulat o magdikit ng anumang bagay sa mga dingding, mesa, upuan, o iba pang bahagi ng silid-aralan. Ang mahuhuli ay maglilinis sa loob ng isang linggo kahit hindi siya cleaner.

9. No bullying of any kind.
Ipinagbabawal ang anumang uri ng pambubully, paninira, o pang-aapi sa kapwa mag-aaral. Ang mahuhuli ay maaaring pagmultahin ng ₱20 at mapatawag ang kanyang mga magulang.

10. Do not bring illegal items such as vape, marijuana, or drugs.
Mahigpit na ipinagbabawal ang pagdadala ng vape, marijuana, droga, at iba pang ipinagbabawal na bagay. Ang mahuhuli ay maaaring i-refer sa Guidance Office at mapatawag ang kanyang mga magulang.

11. Do not leave the classroom without permission.
Bawal lumabas ng silid-aralan nang walang pahintulot mula sa Class President o guro. Ang mahuhuli ay magbabayad ng ₱5 sa Treasurer.

12. No wearing of jewelry, caps, or non-school accessories unless permitted by the school.
Bawal magsuot ng alahas (lalo na ang hikaw para sa mga lalaki), sombrero, at iba pang hindi bahagi ng school uniform habang nasa loob ng paaralan. Ang sinumang hindi susunod ay hindi papayagang pumasok sa silid-aralan hangga't hindi inaalis ang ipinagbabawal na gamit. Kapag lumabag nang higit sa dalawang beses, magbabayad ng ₱5 sa Treasurer.
